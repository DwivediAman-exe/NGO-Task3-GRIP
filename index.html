Help educate Kids for a better tomorrow.

Access to education for children with disabilities is difficult and unsuited to their needs. The inclusive approach encourages students, schools and their staff to be ready to accommodate a disabled child.

Some actions of our organizations in the field:

  • Support for families
  • raining of professionals to provide better support for children with disabilities
  • Contribution to the early detection of diseases which can lead to physical disability
